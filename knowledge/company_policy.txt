COMPANY VACATION POLICY

Employees are entitled to 25 days of paid vacation per year.
Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.
Maximum 10 consecutive days can be taken at once.
Vacation days expire at the end of the calendar year.
Emergency leave can be taken with manager approval.

WORKING HOURS
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
Flexible working hours are available with manager approval.
Remote work is allowed up to 3 days per week.

SICK LEAVE
Employees get 10 sick days per year.
Doctor's note required for absences longer than 3 days.
Notify your manager as soon as possible when calling in sick.
